Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate Case Study
Bike Sharing Data from Cyclistic, Chicago

In this case study, I analyzed the difference in consumer behavior between Member riders and Casual riders of Cyclistic, a Bike Sharing program based in Chicago. The purpose of this study was to devise a way for Cyclistic to convert Casual riders into Member riders. This is the Original Bike Trip Data. It contains data of bike rides from the year 2013, the year of Cyclistic's founding, till the most recently concluded month. The data chosen for this analysis was for the calendar year 2021.
